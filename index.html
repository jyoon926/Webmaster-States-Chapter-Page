    ABOUT US

    Ambitious Learners

    IHSTSA is a family of highly motivated students at Ithaca High School who ambitiously learn about STEM related topics to accomplish great tasks.

    Skilled Leaders

    IHSTSA members earn experience and competence in leadership to aid their road to success in the world of science, technology, art, and math.

    Exceptional Thinkers

    By completing diverse, challenging, and competitive events, the members of IHSTSA have become extraordinary scientists and engineers.

    Activities/
    Fundraising

    IHSTSA members put in a lot of work to be able to compete in regional and national conferences. However, expenses are high, as well as material costs. Donating to our TSA team helps us send as many of our bright and passionate team members as we can.

    Courses at IHS

    At IHS, we offer a variety of courses to encourage the bright minds of today to pursue fields in technology, engineering, and other stem fields. At IHS we encourage students to choose whatever technology classes they find the most interesting, from Design/Drawing for Production, Computer Integrated Manufacturing, Television Production, and much more.

    Learn More

    Leadership

    As some of IHSTSA's most dedicated members, the chapter's officers use their insight and experience with event procedures and standards to direct the team to success. The president, vice president, secretary, treasurer, and reporter, along with the guiding mentership from the advisors, combine their expertise and leadership skills to foster IHSTSA's growing community.
