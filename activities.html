    Crowdfunding

    Members of IHSTSA contact friends, relatives, and even strangers if they would be willing to donate money to TSA through the Ithaca Public Education Initiative (IPEI), an organisation set up in Ithaca, New York, as a way to boost the education and fine arts courses in Ithaca.

    Coffee

    Members of IHSTSA sell pre-ordered coffee, through a fundraising option given by Gimme! Coffee, an Ithaca based company with some locations elsewhere in upstate New York and in New York City, and funds gained from this goes into travel or materials for IHSTSA members, the latter either going into scholarships or general cost lowering.

    Chocolate

    TSA members sell chocolate from Gertrude Hawk, to those in the recent vicinity to produce a small profit which goes into scholarships for those who cannot afford to go to states or nationals, into the general lowering of costs of travel for members of IHSTSA, and materials.
